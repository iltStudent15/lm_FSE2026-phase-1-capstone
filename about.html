About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

From Hartford headquarters to households across all 50 states, Pinnacle Shield Insurance delivers dependable coverage with personal guidance and transparent pricing.

Who We Are

Pinnacle Shield Insurance was founded in 1985 in Hartford, Connecticut, with a simple goal: make insurance protection easier to understand and easier to trust. What began as a regional provider focused on personal auto policies has grown into a nationwide insurance company serving individuals, families, and homeowners in every state.

Today, our headquarters remain in Hartford, CT, where our underwriting, customer support, and claims teams work together to deliver responsive service and practical coverage options. We combine experienced insurance professionals with modern digital tools so customers can compare coverage, estimate premiums, and make informed decisions without unnecessary complexity.

Whether someone is insuring their first vehicle, protecting a longtime home, or planning for their family's future, Pinnacle Shield focuses on clear communication, fair pricing, and long-term relationships. We believe strong coverage should feel dependable before a claim ever happens.
